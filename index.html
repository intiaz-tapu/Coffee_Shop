Coffees

Espresso

A concentrated, strong coffee made by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans under high pressure.

Americano

A popular coffee drink prepared by adding hot water to a single shot of rich, concentrated espresso.

Cappuccino

This classic drink is made with equal parts of espresso, steamed milk, and thick, frothed milk foam.
